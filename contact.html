Meet me!

Hey, I'm Jasmine! I was born and raised in England but have called Dublin my home now for a very long time. I am a very eclectic person, who loves her sweet, sweet doggo & husband. I also love traveling, experiencing new cultures and more importantly, food. Especially the food! In my spare time I like to brunch and dance carelessly to Britney!

Love is a crazy adventure, and I want to capture the ride. I believe that each wedding should be curated to your individuality, so throw the rule book out of the window and make it your own. How I'm I different: For me it's important that we get to know each other and that's why I will always arrange a catch up with you both. I shoot in a documentary style and so it is crucial for you both to feel relaxed around me (I may even use a terrible joke now and then to create some smiles).

I'd like you to think of me as a friend at your wedding here to help create some wicked memories. I will be on hand to assist with all your wedding planning needs and to add a cherry on top of the cake, my full day package includes an engagement shoot. This is a great opportunity to get to know each other and you will have some incredible photos as memories to cherish ahead of your big day.

My work is fun, timeless and creative. On the day you'll find me amongst your guests capturing the day as it happens. Your portrait session will be short and snappy, and don't get worried if I suddenly start climbing a wall or get into a bush! This is all in the name of art! My super relaxed and down to earth approach allows me to capture your day in a candid way. My inspiration is driven by the love of symmetry, with lots of styles and colors (think Wes Anderson film's). I love weddings big and small.

The crazier and quirkier the better!!
